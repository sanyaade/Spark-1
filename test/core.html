Introduction

Hello My name is Oliver Caldwell. I am a 17 year old web developer from south east England.

A little background I started developing about four years ago and have been hooked ever since. I currently work for Rathbone Media, an advertising agency in Chelmsford. I enjoy working with Kohana, a PHP MVC framework, and my own JavaScript library, Spark, although if required then I can use Mootools or jQuery.

This site is a showcase of my work. You can navigate it by clicking any one of these four tabs. Even the effects on this page are examples of my work because they are powered by Spark.

Lost? Just in case the names of the tabs are not descriptive enough for your liking. Portfolio contains previous work and projects, links is a directory of any useful links related to me or my work and contact will allow you to get in touch with me via a fancy e-mail form.
